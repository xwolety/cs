Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does VIP activation take?
A: Usually 5-10 minutes after payment confirmation.

Q: Can I transfer my VIP to another account?
A: VIP is tied to specific authentication. Contact admins for transfer requests.

Q: What happens when VIP expires?
A: You lose access to VIP benefits but keep any purchased items.

Q: Can I get VIP for free?
A: Occasionally through special events or contests. Follow our announcements!

Q: How do I check my remaining VIP time?
A: Contact server admins or check your account on our website.
